Welcome to Galaxypurr Cattery!

Your premier destination for exquisite Silver Shaded British Shorthairs in Kenmore, Washington. As a proud member of The International Cat Association (TICA), we are dedicated to the art of responsible and ethical breeding, ensuring that each feline companion we provide is a testament to the breed's elegance, charm, and distinct personality.

Our Passion and Expertise

At Galaxypurr Cattery, we have cultivated a passion for Silver Shaded British Shorthairs that extends beyond mere appreciation; it's a deep-seated devotion to these remarkable felines. Our family of dedicated breeders brings years of experience and knowledge to the table, ensuring that every kitten we offer is a product of careful selection, rigorous health testing, and a commitment to breed standards.

TICA Certified Excellence

We take immense pride in our TICA certification, which stands as a testament to our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of breeding ethics and practices. Our cattery has met and exceeded TICA's rigorous requirements, assuring you that when you choose a kitten from Galaxypurr Cattery, you're welcoming a pedigree companion of unmatched quality into your home.

Silver Shaded British Shorthairs - Our Specialty

Our specialization in Silver Shaded British Shorthairs sets us apart. These stunning felines, known for their distinctive silver-tipped fur and captivating blue-green eyes, are the epitome of elegance and grace. We have meticulously selected our breeding stock to ensure that each kitten embodies the breed's unique characteristics, including their gentle nature, playful demeanor, and irresistible charm.

Our Mission

At Galaxypurr Cattery, our mission is simple yet profound: to provide loving homes with exceptional Silver Shaded British Shorthair kittens that will enrich lives and bring endless joy. We are dedicated to the well-being of our cats, the satisfaction of our clients, and the preservation of the breed's integrity for generations to come.
